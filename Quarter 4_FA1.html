Dream Big Filipinas

Dream Big Pilipinas is a legally recognized organization that believes that educating young people is the key to brighten the Philippine future. Why should you support this? This group aims to help poor young Filipinos be able to learn how to read and write. Another reason is that it plants to raise money for scholarships and to help stop child labour. Furthermore, a third reason is that along the way, these children will develop a sense of discipline and purpose.
